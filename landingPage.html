Cloud Fellowship Showcase

Human capital is the cornerstone of NOAA’s Innovation and Entrepreneurial Ecosystem. The Fellows program brings together ambitious undergraduate and graduate students who gain deep familiarity with NOAA’s extensive data archives and learn to apply these datasets within cloud-based analytics environments to address real-world climate and weather challenges. Through hands-on data labs integrated into existing curricula, participants explore how to mine, visualize, and model complex atmospheric and oceanographic data streams, equipping them with the technical acumen to drive tomorrow’s innovations.

Goals: Drive new research and foster collaborations that push the boundaries of forecasting, modeling, and analysis by sparking startups and attracting established companies to create jobs, products, and services in the climate and weather sector. By doing this, this Ecosystem can function as a catalyst for transformative economic growth across the north central West Virginia region.
